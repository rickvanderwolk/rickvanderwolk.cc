BOUNCE

A 3D neon arcade experiment, built step by step. Each card is a self-contained version — pick one and play.

01
BOUNCE
The original: a 3D take on classic paddle-and-ball. You vs. the AI in an open box, ball bouncing off all six walls.
02
OBSTACLES
Moving blocks drift through the field. The ball deflects off them, so every rally takes an unpredictable path.
03
SHAPES
Arcade mode: glowing coloured block-shapes weave around the field as obstacles, with waves, streaks and lives.
04
STACK
The mash-up: steer falling pieces with the arrow keys while you keep the rally alive. Locked blocks are obstacles — clear full rows to open the field.
05
2D
The same mash-up flattened to a 2D, classic-arcade look: horizontal paddle-and-ball with a falling-block wall in the middle, scanlines and chip-tune blips.
Three.js, vendored locally · no build step · serve over http(s)
